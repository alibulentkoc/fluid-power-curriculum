# Module 03 — Fluid Fundamentals

> **The machine:** the **Precision Hydraulic Lift Platform** — raises a load to a commanded height and holds it to within ±1 mm.
> **What this module does:** Module 02 gave the platform a complete, connected set of components. But a circuit full of steel does nothing until it is filled. Module 03 chooses the **fluid** that flows through it — and shows why its properties decide whether the platform works.

---

## Where you are in the build

You have a specified, connected component set: a double-acting cylinder, a power unit, lines and ports, a relief valve, a directional valve, and a filter, all wired into one closed loop. Now you fill that loop with the working fluid — the thing that actually carries the power from the pump to the load.

The fluid is not an afterthought. It transmits the power, lubricates every moving part, seals the gaps that hold pressure, and carries away the heat. Choose it well and the platform is smooth, stiff, and long-lived; choose it badly and the same components leak, overheat, sag under load, or wear out. Each lesson takes one property of the fluid, states the decision it forces, and settles it with the platform's own numbers.

1. **The working fluid** — what the fluid must carry and do. *Decision: the fluid's job (power = pressure × flow) and the fluid type.*
2. **Viscosity** — thick enough to seal, thin enough to flow. *Decision: the viscosity grade.*
3. **Bulk modulus** — why the fluid must feel almost solid. *Decision: stiffness, and keeping air out.*
4. **Fluid cleanliness** — keeping the fluid fit for the job. *Decision: the cleanliness target.*

By the end you will have a specified fluid — type, grade, stiffness, and cleanliness — ready for Module 04 to turn into the cylinder's force and speed.
