# Fluid Power Systems Engineering

> A hands-on course that teaches hydraulics by **building one real machine** — a precision hydraulic lift platform — component by component, decision by decision.

## What this course is

Most fluid power courses teach a pile of disconnected formulas. This one teaches a machine. Every lesson takes one real engineering decision about the same platform — *raise a two-tonne load to a commanded height and hold it to within ±1 mm* — and settles it with the physics, a diagram, an interactive demo, a worked example, and a short coding exercise.

By the end you can read, size, and reason about a real hydraulic system, not just recite equations.

## How each lesson works

Every lesson follows the same twelve-part shape:

1. **Why this matters** — the machine, the problem, and the decision.
2. **Physical intuition** — what is really happening, in plain language.
3. **The idea you now need** — the one relationship that settles the decision.
4. **Visual explanation** — a labelled diagram of the real component.
5. **Engineering example** — where this shows up in industry.
6. **Worked example** — given, find, assumptions, solution, result, interpretation.
7. **Interactive demonstration** — a working model you can drive.
8. **Coding exercise** — compute the result yourself.
9. **Knowledge check** — five auto-graded questions.
10. **Challenge problem** — push the idea further.
11. **Common mistakes** — what trips people up.
12. **Key takeaways** — the decision you can now make.

Each lesson also carries an **AI Learning Companion** (prompts to go deeper) and **Global Learning Support** (prompts to study in another language).
